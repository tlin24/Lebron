POWER OR PRECISION, WHO TRULY DEFINES GREATNESS

In the summer of 2016, the basketball world witnessed one of the most dramatic championship series in NBA history as LeBron James and the Cleveland Cavaliers faced off against Stephen Curry and the Golden State Warriors in the 2016 NBA Finals. Golden State entered the series as heavy favorites after a record-breaking 73-win season, and they quickly surged to a commanding 3-1 lead, putting Cleveland on the brink of elimination. However, what followed was a historic turnaround led by LeBron James, who delivered back-to-back 41-point performances in Games 5 and 6, showcasing a level of dominance rarely seen on the Finals stage. As the series shifted back to Oakland for Game 7, tensions were at their peak, with both teams trading blows in a tightly contested battle. In the closing minutes, James made a defining defensive play with a chase-down block on Andre Iguodala, followed shortly by Kyrie Irving's clutch three-pointer that gave Cleveland the edge. The Cavaliers held on to secure a 93-89 victory, completing the first 3-1 comeback in Finals.

ORIGIN

ONE CITY. ONE BEGINNING.

Lebron James was born on December 30, 1984, and Stephen Curry was born on March 14, 1988. Both were born in Akron, Ohio at the same medical facility. This small connection gives their rivalry a strange and memorable starting point. Curry was born there while his father, Dell Curry, played for the Cleveland Cavaliers.

02

BACKGROUND

LEBRON JAMES

The force of control

LeBron was raised by his mother Gloria James in Akron, Ohio. His upbringing was difficult and became part of the motivation that shaped his career and his identity as a father. He entered the NBA with enormous pressure and quickly proved himself as a once in a generation player. His legacy is built on longevity, scoring, playmaking, and the ability to control every part of a game.

STEPHEN CURRY

The force of precision

Curry grew up around the NBA as the son of Dell Curry. Even with that background, he was not seen as a future superstar in the same way LeBron was. Scouts often focused on his size and questioned whether his game would fully translate. He later became one of the most influential players in league history and is widely seen as the greatest shooter ever.

04

LEBRON

LEBRON JAMES

Strengths and weaknesses

LeBron is defined by basketball IQ, playmaking vision, physical dominance, versatility, and durability. He can score, create, and defend across multiple positions. His most discussed weaknesses are free throw shooting, an outside shot that has not always been elite, and a ball dominant style that can narrow how teammates operate around him.

4x NBA Champion

4x MVP

All-time Leading Scorer

CURRY

STEPHEN CURRY

Strengths and weaknesses

Curry is defined by shooting, off ball movement, ball handling, and offensive gravity. He changed the way defenses operate because of how far from the basket he can still threaten a shot. His most discussed weaknesses include size on defense, scoring variance that comes with a jump shooting style, and occasional turnovers under pressure.

4x NBA Champion

2x MVP

TAKEAWAYS

BEYOND THE NUMBERS

KEY TAKEAWAYS

While statistics provide a structured way to compare LeBron James and Stephen Curry, they only capture part of their impact. The charts presented highlight key metrics such as points, rebounds, and assists, but they do not fully represent how each player influences team chemistry, leadership, and overall game flow.

There are also many important factors that extend beyond the scope of this visualization. LeBron's longevity and adaptability have allowed him to remain dominant across decades, ultimately becoming the NBA's all-time leading scorer. On the other hand, Curry's efficiency, three-point shooting, and ability to stretch the floor have reshaped how modern basketball is played.

These perspectives suggest that greatness cannot be defined by a single set of statistics. Instead, it is shaped by a combination of measurable performance and intangible influence, which together continue to define the legacy of these two players.

Numbers tell a story, but impact is what defines greatness.

REFLECTION

PROCESS

REFLECTION

This project explored how editorial design and data visualization can be combined to tell a cohesive narrative. One of the main challenges was balancing visual expression with clarity, especially when integrating animated charts into a structured layout.

Implementing interactions such as line drawing and bar animations required multiple iterations, as achieving the intended behavior while maintaining consistency across all charts proved to be more complex than expected. Simplifying certain animations ultimately allowed the visuals to remain clear and aligned with the overall design direction.

This process highlighted the importance of restraint in design. While interactive elements can enhance engagement, they must support the content rather than distract from it. Moving forward, I would further refine the interaction system and explore more seamless ways to integrate motion into the narrative.

Overall, this project strengthened my ability to translate conceptual ideas into structured visual systems, while reinforcing the balance between aesthetics, usability, and storytelling.
